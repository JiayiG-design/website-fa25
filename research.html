Research Approach

My research is grounded in human-centered design principles and employs a variety of qualitative and quantitative methods to understand user needs, behaviors, and experiences. I believe in the power of research to inform design decisions and create more meaningful, accessible, and effective solutions.

Each research project begins with a clear question or hypothesis, followed by careful methodology design, data collection, and analysis. The insights gained from this work not only contribute to academic knowledge but also directly inform practical design applications and industry best practices.
